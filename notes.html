About Us

We are also students who faced and experienced the pain and struggle of Agriculture students for the materials and resourses, So this idea comes up and we are here to help all our student community by providing a Sarovar for all materials/resources of B.SC Agriculture.

Our Vision

We are here to provide all the materials and resourses related to B.Sc Agriculture (Notes,Assignments,Practicals etc.) at a single place. Which will save your time in searching across the globe coz you are getting all of them here and Will makes your studing experience more easy.
